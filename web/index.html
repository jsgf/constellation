Constellation

As seen at Maker Faire!

Constellation gives particpants the ability to create, move and destroy stars, and be part of a network of constellations. It operates interactively in real time, from a live video feed.

Constellation is one application of the Bokchoi interactive video manipulation system. Visual effects can be easily constructed by writing programs in the Lua scripting language.
